Using the Tool to create custom prioritizations

Regional Aquatic Prioritization and Mapping Tool. Go to 'Create New > Watershed Prioritization Scenario' to start. From here you can enter:

  • The goal or proportion of habitat to conserve for each species.

  • The importance weighting which indicates the relative importance of each species.

  • The inclusion of prioritization constraints such as climate change, invasives and watershed condition.

The process will run in the background; you can continue to use the application and will be notified when the processing is completed. At that time you can click the 'refresh' button to view the results of the analysis. Selecting the analysis and clicking 'Attributes' will provide the details of the analysis and the resulting reserve scenario.

Learn More

There are many other aspects to the Regional Aquatic Prioritization and Mapping Tool to explore. You can export scenarios to GIS, iteratively Edit them to fine-tune the prioritization, collaborate and share scenarios with others and view underlying data layers.
