Welcome to the Watershed Priorities Tool

An aquatic habitat restoration decision support system for Oregon, Washington, and Idaho

Step 1: Sign In

If you already have an account, Sign In and enter your username and password.

If you need an account, click Register and follow the instructions on screen.

Step 2: Review Regional Prioritizations

Click the Shared tab and find the Regional Prioritizations.

Click the checkbox to load the map and view the Attributes page to see detailed results.

Step 3: Create a Custom Prioritization

Click Create New then Watershed Prioritization Analysis to define your prioritization scenario.

Follow the steps to define your focal species goals and prioritization constraints.

Step 4: Review Results

Highlight your prioritization in the table of contents and click Attributes.

From here you can evaluate which watersheds were selected and how well species goals were met.

Looking for more info? Click "help" in the upper-right for more documentation.
